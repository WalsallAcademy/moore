Moore's Law

Gordon Moore was a brilliant engineer that helped create Intel, back in the 1960s.
Intel is now one of the largest computer chip-making companies in the world.

In 1965 he noticed that the number of transistors in a computer chip was doubling roughly every two years, and predicted that it would carry on. (He was right)

This prediction came to be knows as 'Moore's Law'.
